About Us

Established in 2015, Fetty Farms is directly related to Fetty Homes and Properties Limited and is set to transform farming in and out of Nigeria.

Fetty Farm is committed to growing and empowering the community by providing new opportunities for farmers and freshly farmed healthy food for the nation.

Fetty Farms is the fastest growing agribusiness group in the southwestern region of Nigeria and is involved in extensive farming, with key focus on:

  • Oil palm tree cultivation
  • Palm oil processing and production
  • Livestock rearing (cows for beef production)
  • Maize, wheat and cassava trading (cultivation, milling, processing, production and packaging)
  • Pineapple plantation

Vision

To create a strong farming network that promotes the values of healthy living by providing our growing client network with freshly farmed non genetically modified food that is healthy and farmed in an environmentally friendly, self sustainable environment

Mission

Providing freshly farmed food that caters to the needs of all our end users of giving them access to healthy food

Products

Oil Palm

Our Palm Oil is derived from environmentally sustainable and socially responsible sources

Cultivation & Processing

We have advanced oil palm cultivation methods ensuring ample irrigation to all our growing and already fruitful trees in order to yield the highest quality palm fruit for palm oil production

Palm Oil Production

Our palm oil and palm kernel oil products are processed on the farm by a highly skilled and experienced team that is well educated on the use of all the machinery, therefore we are able to confidently state that the highest quality standards are met in the production of our oil

CATTLE RANCH

Our ranch specializes in cattle husbandry and is focused on selectively breeding the highest grade of organically raised cattle that in turn produce the highest quality of meat

MAIZE, WHEAT & CASSAVA

We are ideally located in areas that provide a great natural climate for cultivation of maize and cassava. We also handle the processing creating high end quality products such as fresh maize, garri, kpokpuru and semolina.
